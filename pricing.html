How does the monthly price work?

Pricing is based on the number of workers that you pay in a given month. It's simple — every month you pay a base fee of $100, plus $6 per employee.

For example, a twenty person company would pay $220 each month. This includes the entire platform — even quarterly and annual tax filings, W-2s, and 1099s.

Are there additional costs for employees in multiple states?

No. Our simple monthly price is the same no matter of how many states your employees are in or how many locations your business has.

Does it cost more to pay employees weekly?

No. You can run payroll as many times per month as you need. Our straightforward monthly price includes unlimited payroll runs. You can even set up as many payroll schedules as you'd like at no additional cost.

How does the six-month money back guarantee work?

It’s easy. Try us out, see what you think, and if you don’t like our system, we’ll return your money.

We know you are going to be impressed with how easily Employii automates your HR, payroll, and time tracking process, but if for some reason you are not satisfied we’ll refund your entire six months.

Is there a contract?

No. Everything is month-to-month.
